Mollies

Habitat

- Molly fish prefer to live in shallow surface waters.

- In the wild, they are usually found in loosely vegetated coastal streams.

- However, these fish are known to do well in lakes, estuaries, deltas, swamps, ponds, marshes and even in ephemeral places like roadside ditches.

- They prefer to stay under floating vegetation or near structures to stay out of view of predators.

- Ideally, they like water with a pH range of between 7.5 and 8.5 and a temperature of between 64 and 82 Â°F.

Feeding

Molly fish are technically omnivores because they not only consume plant materials but are also known to consume:

- Crustaceans.

- Insectes.

- Worms.
