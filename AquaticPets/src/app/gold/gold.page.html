Goldfish

Habitat

- In the wild, goldfish can be found in slow-moving, freshwater bodies of water.

- As with their close relative the carp, they thrive in slightly murky water.

- In captivity, an aquarium with live plants and a dirt bottom is ideal.

- Bi-weekly water changes are a good idea as a goldfish tank is hard to keep clean.

Feeding

1) In captivity, goldfish are commonly fed:

- dried flake.

- pellet food.

2) Good diet supplements include:

- freeze dried Tubifex worms.

- mosquito larva.

- bloodworms.

- daphnia.

- brineshrimp.

- vegetation such as boiled peas and lettuce.
