Betta Fish

Habitat

- Bettas live in rice paddies and river basins.

- Their natural territory is about three-feet square.

- The areas in which they live are relatively shallow and thick with vegetation.

- The streams are slow-moving and in the dry season can easily evaporate to almost nothing.

Feeding

- Insects.

- Insect Larvae.

- Plant Material.

- Flying Bugs

- Bloodworms
