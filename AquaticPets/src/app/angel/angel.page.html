Angelfish

Habitat

- Angelfish are native to a large area of tropical South America, including much of the Amazon River system.

- In their natural habitat, they are found almost exclusively in quiet, slow moving water.

- In the wild they prefer dimly lit areas, under overhanging vegetation or among trees that have fallen into the river.

Feeding

- Worms.

- Small Crustaceans.

- Aqueon Tropical Flakes.

- Color Flakes.

- Tropical Granules.

- Shrimp Pellets.
