Chilodonella

Symptoms

- Dulling of the colours due to excessive slime, frying of the fins, weakness, gill damage.

- This disease causes a blue white cloudiness on the skin and attacks the gills.

- Later the skin may be broken down and the gills destroyed.

- The fish may behave as if they have irritations.

Treatment

- Acriflaving (trypaflaving) may be used at 1% solution (5 ml per litre).

- As acriflavine can sterilize fish, the water should be gradually changed after a cure has been effected.

- It also helps to raise the temperature to about 80 Â°F.
